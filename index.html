CyberSecurity Threats

What Are Cybersecurity Threats

Cybersecurity focuses on threats, vulnerabilities, and attacks. Threats are pontential harmful events, vulnerabilities are weaknesses making targets easy prey, and attacks exploit these weaknesses.

The Importance Of CyberSecurity.

The internet is a vital part in our lives, used by sectors like healthcare and finance. Protecting the data on it is crucial for our safety and economy.

This is where Cybersecurity plays a role in keeping our internet and data safe from harm. Cybersecurity is essential for maintaining the integrity, confidentialty, availability of digital information, systems, and networks in our network world.
